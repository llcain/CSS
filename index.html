CSS practice

When selectors are combined they should be read from right to left. The selector farthest to the right, directly before the opening curly bracket, is known as the key selector.

The first combined selector above, .hotdog p, includes two selectors: a class and a type selector.

The only difference between the second selector and the first selector is the addition of the class selector of mustard to the end of the paragraph type selector.
